Biography

Dr. Rahul Bodhi is an Assistant Professor of Organizational Behavior and Human Resource Management at the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Sirmaur, India. He holds a PhD in Management from the School of Management Studies, Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, India.

He worked as a Visiting Research Fellow at the Graduate Institute of Digital Learning and Education, National Taiwan University of Science & Technology (NTUST), Taipei, Taiwan. He also served as a Visiting Faculty at the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT), Kolkata, India. He has conducted management development programs (MDPs) and training sessions for banks and other financial institutions. His research interests lie in the Future of Work, Cyber Behavior, Incivility, and other Behavioral Outcomes. He has guided MBA and PhD scholars in cyber behaviour and well-being; one doctoral student has been awarded her PhD.

He is also a reviewer for leading international publishers and reputed conferences such as Elsevier, Emerald, Springer, Sage, Wiley, and the Academy of Management (AOM) and European Academy of Management (EUARM). His research appears in various leading international journals, including the International Journal of Manpower, Journal of Knowledge Management, Information Technology and People, International Journal of Hospitality Management, Journal of Business Research, Evidence-Based HRM, Journal of Intellectual Capital, Technological Forecasting & Social Change, among others.
